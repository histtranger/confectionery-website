About Us

Established around 1985, Beau's Bonbons has been a go-to for people with sweet tooths for 31 years. Beau Sr. is a master when it comes to the art of candy making, and taught all he knows to his son, Beau Jr. who now runs the shop today. Beau Sr. always wanted to have a variety of sweet things for his customers to taste and experience, so he hand makes the same candy he did 31 years ago, as well as stocking everyone's favorite brands and candies in his store.

Sweets

  • Gummy Candy

    Chewy, sometimes sweet and sometimes sour, but always a crowd pleaser! We offer a colorful array of some favorites like gummy worms, bears, fruit, and a whole lot more!

  • Chocolate Stuff

    Candy coated, caramel filled, mint flavored, we've got it all! Beau Sr. and Beau Jr. prepare handmade chocolate treats, as well as stock all your favorite brands and snacks.

  • Favorite Brands

    We offer your favorite brands ranging from Charleston Chew to Smarties to Warheads. We offer a wide variety of classic candy, a sure fire way to get a blast from the past.

  • Homemade Products

    Fudge, taffy, caramel, chocolate, lollipops, and more! Beau Sr. and Beau Jr. and their team of skilled candy makers make homemade treats for you to take home everyday! Need a special order? Ask one of our employees and they will help you with all the preparations.
